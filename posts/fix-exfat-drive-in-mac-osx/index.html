Fix Exfat Drive in Mac Osx

Mar 9, 2024

source: gist - scottopell/fix_exfact_drive.md

Fix:

  1. diskutil list
  2. get ID (eg: disk1s1)
  3. sudo fsck_exfat -d <ID>
  4. enter YES for prompt
  5. drive will be mounted
